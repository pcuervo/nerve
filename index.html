< Something to say >

“Few are those who see with their own eyes and feel with their own hearts” Albert Einstein once remarked. As was so often this case with him, he was onto something.

Today we are totally deluged with information about the world we live in and the times in which we move. But despite the pluralisation delivered by social media, the vast majority of that information still actually comes from the same places, passing through the same filters of un-lived experience and vested interest, and still does very little to enrich our lives, expand our experience or meaningfully inform us.

Fear prevails as the news current; but questioning of big, systemic issues remains too elusive. The pantomime of opinion battles carried out in the various territories & timelines of social media often fog a finer comprehension of the extraordinary realities & complexities of life within and without. A majority of people are routinely depicted as a psychology-less shoal to be condemned or saved, subject to the partisan bias of a given voice.

The collective of writers, artists, academics and other alert people who have come together to make NERVE happen are pretty certain there is another way of doing information, and a more nourishing, enjoyable & entertaining way of looking at modern life, current affairs and learning new stuff in an active, authentic and informed way: that information can be voluminous and textured.

We feel as though the status quo creates a cyclical, reductive & limiting illusion about the present and the future and we’d like to try and create a space where that illusion is thinnest.

There are cracks in the mainstream corporate media and in projects like “The Correspondent” in Holland to the Bristol Cable here in the UK we see a joined up insistence for independent, autonomous high quality writing, film and audio. It’s our conviction – based on a load of broad evidence – that a big appetite exists for exactly this type of pluralised, creative, investigative and original information. As does a desire for involvement.

We want to invite you to join with us as partners in this experiment to see where we can take it. Initially via two regular pod-cast formats & a short film channel (please have a look at the NERVE Manifesto) we hope to build out and develop a wider platform that many more people can contribute to and that can take many more forms …. forms that we have not yet even imagined.

NERVE Studio will be organised along the following governing principles:

Material

Two core elements:
Pod cast channel
Short film

Audio Podcasts

to be in three distinct formats: An open/independent inlet, documentary & long-form interview:

“HINTERLAND”
Productions of varying length solely from independent contributors
“oUR”
Fifty-nine minute doc-cast with emphasis on latitudinous material and original sound design
“The Card Game”
Long form interview

Visual Short Film

to be in the form of documentary and occasionally, short form drama/fiction.

All visual documentaries to be protagonist (not media “witness”) led and thus not featuring any guiding voice-over or external shaping.

All material to be autonomously developed; seeking to extend, enhance and challenge listener/viewer understanding.

All material to be delivered via the highest possible and most radical/idiosyncratic creative processes and production styles.

The “HINTERLAND” strand will be entirely dedicated to ideas and concepts from open contribution/submission, which we will support andhelp to produce. No content on this strand will be made by the NERVE collective.

Funding

No advertising (or advertising revenue) in any form will be accepted on either the host platform or the individual content programmes.

To retain total editorial and creative independence, the funding formula will be predicated around the following streams:

Licensed syndication

of certain material to established independent, global media outlets

Full Subscription

membership – to be offered 6 months from launch - “NERVE FIBRES” Arena – payment for full 12 month access to extended material and exclusive episodes

Partners Arena:

an engaged community of listeners/viewers making small, regular or one off donations to retain independence and ongoing platform development.

Merchandise:

exclusive range produced by associated artists on public sale.

One off special “NERVE LIVE” events

produced and presented by artists, writers and musicians from the contributing NERVE collective.

Model

A not-for-profit, arts/media based social enterprise in perpetuity.

All material produced by the NERVE collective of writers, artists, musicians & film-makers.

A dedicated open channel for the generation of new material and ideas.
